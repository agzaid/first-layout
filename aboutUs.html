Who We Are

Bin Sawad for Batteries Company was established in 2005 in accordance with the Saudi Companies Law as a limited liability company. One of the largest companies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Battery sales: 1,100,000 batteries per year. Oil sales: 1,800,000 cartons per year. Tires sales: 1,300,000 tires per year. We have more than 2,200 customers inside the Kingdom, and some customers are the same for batteries, tires, and oils, and this is also helping us. We have 84 branches inside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and 40 distributors inside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Bin Sawad specialized first in batteries and oils, and after that in tires. We have been doing business with tires for 13 years in the local market. Bin Sawad has been importing tires for 3 years.
